A Little About Us

The Pastry Studio is a small-batch bakery rooted in the Eastern Panhandle, where every pastry is handcrafted to order. No factory lines, no shortcuts, just butter, patience, and a love for the craft.

From flaky morning pastries to showstopping custom cakes, each bake leaves the studio fresh, beautiful, and made just for you.
